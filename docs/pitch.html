The Problem It Solves

Nigerian schools today run on paper, WhatsApp groups, and manual processes. This creates real problems:

  • Attendance is paper-based — 500+ records daily, easily lost, impossible to audit
  • Timetables take 3-5 days of manual work and often have clashes
  • Duty assignments are confusing — staff don't know their duties, no one tracks completion
  • Communications are scattered — WhatsApp groups, notice boards, word of mouth
  • No real-time visibility — school leaders can't see who's present, who's on duty, or what's pending
  • No accountability — tasks assigned informally, no audit trail
In short: Running a Nigerian secondary school is like flying a plane with no instruments. AFCS Smart Campus provides the cockpit.

The Solution — 12 Integrated Modules

1. Smart Attendance

QR code + manual check-in/out for staff and students. Late detection, class breakdowns, real-time dashboard.

2. Duty Roster Engine

8 duty types (Morning Talk, Assembly, Sanitary, etc.). Auto-rotation so assignments are fair and nobody is forgotten.

3. AI Timetable Generator

Creates conflict-free timetables in seconds. No more 3-day manual scheduling marathons.

4. Muster Parade

Digital parade sessions, briefings, task assignment with acknowledgement. Everything tracked.

5. Prefect Roles

18 prefect roles assignable to students. Badges, filters, leadership tracking.

6. Daily Reports

Structured reports with AI summaries. Know what happened, what's challenging, and what's next.

7. Telegram Bot

25+ commands. Staff check duties, tasks, timetables from their phone. Zero cost to run.

8. Multi-Channel Comms

Telegram + WhatsApp + SMS. Falls back automatically if one channel fails.

9. Automation Engine

13 scheduled rules. Reminders, alerts, digests — all sent automatically.

10. AI Assistant

Natural language chat. Ask "Who hasn't checked in?" or "What's today's duty?" and get instant answers.

11. Global Search

Press Ctrl+K, search any feature, navigate instantly. Like Spotlight for your school.

12. Notifications Hub

Full history of all notifications. Who was notified, when, and whether they received it.
